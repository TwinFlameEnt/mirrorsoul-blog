To play the slice of MirrorSoul game, follow these steps:

### Open the Game Page
Begin by navigating to the game's URL: [MirrorSoul Demo Slice](http://demo.mirrorsoul.srv1.tuchacloud.ru/)

- Open your preferred web browser (such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
- In the address bar, type or paste the following URL: [MirrorSoul Demo Slice](#). Make sure its `http` not `https`. We're currently working on secure websocket streaming so use unsecure connection for now.

### Playing the Game
With the game started, familiarize yourself with the controls:

- Movement: Game involves character or object movement, use the arrow keys or <keyboard>W/A/S/D</keyboard> keys on your keyboard or Touchstick on mobile device.
- Look: Use mouse or touch sticks to look around. Hold `RMB` to aim.
- Interaction: Use the E on interactive elements.

> Gameplay Tips: Pay attention to on-screen prompts or tooltips, as they often provide valuable guidance on game mechanics and objectives.

### Options to adjust settings:

- Sound: Toggle sound effects and music on or off.
- Full-Screen Mode: For an immersive experience, switch to full-screen mode, typically by pressing F11 or using the in-game menu.

### Troubleshooting
- Game Not Loading: Ensure your browser is up to date, and disable any ad blockers or extensions that might interfere with the game.
- Enjoy your gaming experience! For any technical issues or further assistance, consider visiting the support page of the game or reaching out to the developers.
